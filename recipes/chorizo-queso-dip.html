Chorizo Queso Dip

The flavor of this spicy chorizo queso dip definitely sets it apart from the rest. Great for a party or when you can’t figure out what to do with a roll of bulk chorizo sausage, this dip is rich, creamy, and packed with bold, smoky flavor. As the spicy chorizo infuses the melted cheeses, the diced tomatoes with green chilies add a subtle tang and just the right amount of heat, balancing the richness of the cream cheese and Velveeta. Perfect for game day, potlucks, or a cozy night in, this crowd-pleasing dip is guaranteed to disappear fast!

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces bulk chorizo sausage
  • 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers (such as Rotel), drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, cubed
  • 1 (8 ounce) package processed cheese (such as Velveeta), cubed

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir chorizo in the hot skillet until well browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain well and transfer to a slow cooker.
  2. Add diced tomatoes with chiles, cream cheese, and processed cheese to slow cooker; stir well. Cover and cook on Low until cheese has melted, stirring occasionally, about 1 ½ to 2 hours.
